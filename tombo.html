Fiji Tombo

H&N Foods -- The Fresh Tuna Experts -- announces the steady availability of fresh Fiji Tombo (albacore) ahi from the pure, deep waters of the South Pacific. We're one of the largest fresh tuna buyers in the world. That's why we sent our buyers to Fiji, where longliners fish the big albacore that swim in the pure, deep waters of the South Pacific. At about half the price of yellowfin and bigeye tuna, our Fiji Tombo has an attractive deep pink meat, making it a great value. We offer both H&G Fiji Tombo and Loins (skinless, bloodline removed). Call us today and we'll show you how our Fiji Tombo can put new sizzle in your fresh tuna sales.
