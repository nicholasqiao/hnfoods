Community

Since inception, H&N Foods International has carefully balanced its growth in the seafood industry with mindful support for the conservation of ocean wildlife and the marine ecosystem. Our memberships and active involvement with the National Fisheries Institute and the California Fisheries Institute constitutes H&N Foods' commitment to the continued success and progress for all of our vendors, partners and customers in the global seafood community. These organizations have been staunch advocates of the fish and seafood industry representing our marketplace in the U.S Congress and other regulatory agencies. H&N Foods' affiliations with these institutes helps ensure healthy fishery populations and resources while protecting the business interests of companies in the fish and seafood community.

Our commitment to industry-friendly legislation and the success of the industry in general is tempered with our concern to preserve that which makes our business possible--ocean wildlife, the marine ecosystem and community involvement. Our support of Ocean Trust and the California Academy of Sciences, as well as the establishment of the H&N Foundation, exemplifies our understanding of the obligations we have to people in the local communities in which we work. Each of these organizations proliferate the knowledge, research and environmental enhancements necessary to fortify the ocean's bounty for future generations. As farmers of the sea, H&N Foods embraces the lesson to be learned from the age-old adage, "You reap what you sow".
